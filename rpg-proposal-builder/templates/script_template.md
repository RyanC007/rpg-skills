## HANDLING COMMON QUESTIONS

**"Why do you require 3 months upfront for content?"**
"Consistent content takes time to compound. One month of blogs doesn't move the needle. Three months is the minimum for your strategy to start building the SEO and GEO signals that get you found. We require it upfront because partial execution doesn't produce results — and we're accountable for yours."

**"What if we only want the Backpack for now?"**
"The Backpack requires a content plan. That's not a upsell — it's how the system works. A strategy without execution doesn't compound. The minimum is Backpack plus three months of content. That's $2,250 to start."

**"What does 'GEO optimization' mean?"**
"GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It's how you get found by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI overviews — not just traditional search. It's increasingly where discovery happens. We bake it into everything we build."
