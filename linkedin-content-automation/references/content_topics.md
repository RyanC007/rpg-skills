## Post Angle Templates

### Educational Posts
- "Here's what most founders get wrong about [topic]..."
- "3 things I learned about [topic] the hard way..."
- "The real cost of [common mistake]..."
- "How to [achieve result] without [common expensive solution]..."

### Story-Based Posts
- "When we got laid off in 2024, we learned..."
- "A client just asked me about [topic]. Here's what I told them..."
- "I used to think [misconception]. Then I discovered..."

### Tactical Posts
- "Quick win: How to [specific action] in under [time]..."
- "Step-by-step: [Process] that actually works..."
- "The [tool/framework] we use to [achieve result]..."

### Thought Leadership Posts
- "Hot take: [controversial but defensible opinion]..."
- "Everyone talks about [popular topic], but nobody mentions [overlooked aspect]..."
- "The future of [industry trend] for small businesses..."

### Engagement Posts
- "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
- "Unpopular opinion: [statement]. Am I wrong?"
- "Poll: Which would you choose? [Option A] or [Option B]?"

## Infographic Ideas

When suggesting infographic posts, consider these formats:

1. **Process Diagrams**: Visual workflows (e.g., "The Founders Flywheel in 4 Steps")
2. **Comparison Charts**: Side-by-side comparisons (e.g., "AI Tool vs. AI Strategy")
3. **Statistics**: Data visualization (e.g., "ROI of Marketing Automation by Business Size")
4. **Checklists**: Visual checklists (e.g., "5 Signs Your Business Needs AI Automation")
5. **Before/After**: Transformation visuals (e.g., "Marketing Before vs. After Automation")

## Content Mix Guidelines

Maintain variety across post types:
- 40% Educational/Tactical
- 30% Thought Leadership
- 20% Story-Based/Personal
- 10% Engagement/Questions

Rotate through topic areas to avoid repetition:
- Don't post about the same topic two days in a row
- Vary between tactical and strategic content
- Mix short, punchy posts with longer, in-depth posts
